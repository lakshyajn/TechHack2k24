About TechHack 2024

Our Mission

TechHack 2024 is a 48-hour hackathon aimed at fostering innovation, collaboration, and technological advancement. Our mission is to bring together the brightest minds from around the world to tackle real-world challenges and create groundbreaking solutions.

What to Expect

  • 48 hours of non-stop hacking
  • Exciting challenges from industry leaders
  • Workshops and mentorship sessions
  • Networking opportunities with tech professionals
  • Amazing prizes for top projects

Who Can Participate

TechHack 2024 is open to students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts aged 15-30. Whether you're a coding wizard, a design guru, or a business visionary, there's a place for you at our hackathon!
